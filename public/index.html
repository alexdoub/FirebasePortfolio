Jane

Jane is an online womens fashion and apparel store similar to Amazon.com. They also feature daily deals and flash sales with unbeatable prices.

Jane had an iOS app and they needed an Android app to match it, so I worked with their designer and one other developer to create the entire app from the ground up. The Android app uses the PayPal SDK for secure online payments, neatly connects to their existing web service, and even supports special layouts for tablets.

  • Store: Google Play
  • Date: Spring 2015
  • Client: Jane

Sudo

Sudo is a semi anonymous telephony, SMS and email app for protecting your privacy. You can create 'pseudo' identities to hide your main phone number and email.

I contributed to this app by adding several new features to their existing codebase for the release of a new version. Additions include email attachment functionality, updated identity card views, several new animations, and changes to the phone calling screen.

  • Store: iTunes
  • Date: Summer-Fall 2016
  • Client: Anonyome Labs

Prosperworks

ProsperWorks is a beautiful, easy-to-use CRM designed to help every salesperson excel at their job. It is Googles #1 CRM and is included in Google's G-Suite list of recommended products for productivity. It seamlessly integrates with your Gmail account through a convenient chrome addon to help you manage your customer data.

Prosperworks needed both an iOS and Android app for their existing web service and I worked with a small team of their developers and designers to create the initial Android release from the ground up.

  • Store: Google Play
  • Date: Spring-Summer 2015

Time Machine

Time Machine is a lifestyle app where you can find creative ways to spend your time while supporting causes and brands you care about. Share your actions on social media to spread the word and have a chance to win prizes.

Time Machine was an iOS and Android project which was forked into several spinoffs withs different names and themes. I was responsible for most of the client side coding for the iOS and Android app versions. However, since the project's timeframe has since passed, the app and its services have been discontinued.

  • Horses Help: Google Play, iTunes
  • Date: Spring & Winter 2016, Spring 2017
